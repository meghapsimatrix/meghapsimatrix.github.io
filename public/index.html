Call for SIG Officer Nominations

Our Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis SIG is seeking new officers for the leadership team, with terms starting after the end of the 2025 convention next April. We welcome nominations (including self-nominations!) for any of the following positions: Program Chair – organizes and manages the proposal review process and assembles the SIG’s program for the annual convention. Secretary – reports on SIG activities, including the annual business meeting, and leads communications with SIG members.
