Our Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis SIG is seeking new officers for the leadership team, with terms starting after the end of the 2025 convention next April. We welcome nominations (including self-nominations!) for any of the following positions:

  • Program Chair – organizes and manages the proposal review process and assembles the SIG’s program for the annual convention.
  • Secretary – reports on SIG activities, including the annual business meeting, and leads communications with SIG members.
  • Treasurer – manages and reports on SIG financial accounts, approves any expenditures.
  • Graduate student member-at-large – engages with graduate students around SIG activities, provides input and represents student perspectives on SIG activities.

Per the SIG bylaws, our current program co-chairs, Carlton Fong and Derek Rodgers, will be stepping into the role of SIG co-chairs. If you are interested in running for any of the open positions, please contact James Pustejovsky no later than October 20th, 2024.

Getting involved as a SIG officer is a great way to contribute to our community. For most positions, the duties are light and the time commitment is low and flexible. The program chair responsibilities do require a bit more time and effort, mostly during Summer and early Fall, but the position also offers a meaningful and fulfilling way to shape our community’s priorities and direction. Program chair responsibilities can also be split between two co-chairs, so you are welcome to run with a buddy. If you have questions or are interested in learning more about any of the positions, please feel free to reach out to any of ther current SIG officers. You can also read more about the official responsibilities of each position in our SIG bylaws.
